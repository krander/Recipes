Strawberry Spritzer

Yields: 2 1/2 quarts

  • 1 package frozen strawberries, thawed (10 oz)
  • 2 liters lemon-lime soda
  • 1 can frozen pink lemonade concentrate, thawed (12 oz)

Directions

  1. Place strawberries in a blender
  2. Cover and process until pureed
  3. Pour into a large pitcher
  4. Stir in sode and lemonade concentrate
  5. Serve immediately
