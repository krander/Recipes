Pizza Tree Ranch

Yields 3 cups

  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 teaspoon Kosher salt
  • 5 black peppercorns
  • 2 medium garlic gloves
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill (optional)
  • 1 pinch paprika
  • 1 1/2 c vegetable oil
  • 3 scallions, sliced thin
  • 1 1/2 cup buttermilk

Directions

  1. Combine egg, salt, peppercorns, garlic, water, and paprika
  2. Puree until finely ground
  3. Add oil very slowly while stirring. If emulsion breaks, don't fret! Start over with 1 egg yolk and 1 tablespoon of water. Add to broken emulsion very slowly
  4. After adding all oil, add buttermilk, scallions, and dill
