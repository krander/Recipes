Hole-y Cake

  • 1 box German chocolate cake mix
  • 1 jar caramel (19.5 oz)
  • 1 jar hot fudge
  • 1/2 package health bits

Directions

  1. Mix cake as directed, pour into 9 x 13 pan
  2. Bake as directed
  3. While cake is still how, poke holes in cake with wooden spoon handle
  4. Pour jar of caramel and fudge into holes
  5. Cool and refridgerate
  6. Once cooled, sprinkle heath bits on top
