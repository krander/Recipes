Cake Mix Cookies

From Grandma Anderson

  • 1 package cake mix of your choice (18 1/2 oz)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon flavoring or vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mix-in of your choice (i.e. nuts, raisins, chocolate chips, peanut butter, butterscotch, cinnamon chips, health bits) (optional)

Directions

  1. Combine half the mix with eggs, oil, and flavoring with electric mixer
  2. Beat until smooth
  3. Stir in remaining mix plus the mix-in
  4. Drop on ungreased cookie sheets
  5. Bake at 375 ° , centers will be soft about 9-10 minutes
  6. Allow to cool before removing 1-2 minutes

Reccommended combos

  • Vanilla cake mix + health bits
  • Spice cake mix + cinnamon chips
  • Chocolate cake mix + mint or chocolate chips
