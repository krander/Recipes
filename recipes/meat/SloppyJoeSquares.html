Sloppy Joe Squares

Serves: 6-8

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 8 oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 envelope sloppy joe mix
  • 1 teaspoon instant minced onion
  • 2 packages refrigerated crescent rolls
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 °
  2. Brown ground beef in large skillet over medium-high head
  3. Drain and return to skillet
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, sloppy joe mix, water, and onion
  5. Bring to boil
  6. Reduce hear and simmer for 10 minutes
  7. Unroll 1 package of crescent rolls, fit into bottom of lightly greased 9 x 13 baking disk, pressing seams together
  8. Sprinkle ground beef mixture over dough, sprinkle with cheese
  9. Unroll remaining package of crescent rolls, pressing seams together and place over cheese
  10. Bake for 15 minutes or until top is golden brown
