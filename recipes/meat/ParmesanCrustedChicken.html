Parmesan Crusted Chicken

  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 cup dried bread crumbs
  • 1/3 cup parmesan, grated
  • 2 tablespoon parsley, chopped, fresh
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 large pinch of italian seasoning
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 lb chicken, cut into 1-2 inch pieces

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 °
  2. Pat chicken dry
  3. Stir garlic and butter together
  4. Stir together bread crumbs, parmesan, parsley, salt, garlic salt, italian seasoning, and black pepper
  5. Dip chicken into garlic and butter mixture, then breadcrumb mixture
  6. Place chicken on baking sheet. Drizzle on remaining garlic butter
  7. Bake for 15 minutes
