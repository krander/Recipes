Hearty Stuffed Bell Peppers

Serves: 4

  • 1/3 cup seasoned rice with tomato and basil
  • 4 red peppers
  • 10 oz ground beef
  • 1 1/2 cups 100% vegetable juice
  • 3 tablespoon parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper

Directions

  1. Cook rice, omitting butter or oil, cool for 10 minutes
  2. Cut top inch off of peppers and remove seeds
  3. Combine cooked rice, ground beef, 1/4 cup of vegetable juice, cheese, salt, and pepper in a bowl
  4. Spoon mixture into peppers, add to saucepan
  5. Pour remaining 1 1/4 cup vegetable juice around peppers, set pan over medium heat
  6. Bring juice to boil
  7. Reduce hear, cover, and simmer until peppers are tender and beef if cooked (20-25 minutes)
