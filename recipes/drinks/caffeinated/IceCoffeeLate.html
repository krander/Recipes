IcedCoffeeLate

Yields: 8 serving

  • 1/2 cup instant coffee granules
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 4 cup chocolate milk
  • 2 cup cold water
  • 1 can sweetened, condensed milk (14 oz)
  • Ice cubes

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, dissolve coffee in boiling water
  2. Stir in the chocolate milk, cold water, and condensed milk
  3. Serve over ice
