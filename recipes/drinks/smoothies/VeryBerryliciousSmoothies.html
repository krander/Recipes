Very Berry-Licious Smoothies

Yields: 6 servings

  • 2 cup cranberry juice
  • 4 cup frozen strawberries
  • 2 cup frozen raspberries
  • 1 1/2 cup raspberry yogurt

Directions

  1. Place all ingredients in a blender
  2. Cover and process until smooth
  3. Pour into chilled glasses
  4. Serve immediately
