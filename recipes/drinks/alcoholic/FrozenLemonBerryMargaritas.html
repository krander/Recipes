Frozen Lemon-Berry Margaritas

Yields: 4 servings

  • 4 lime wedges
  • 2 tablespoon coarse sugar
  • 2/3 cup thawed lemonade concentrate
  • 1 cup frozen raspberries
  • 2 cup ice cubes
  • 2 packages frozen sliced strawberries, thawed (10 oz each)
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup tequila

Directions

  1. Using lime wedges, moisten the rims of four margarita or cocktail glasses
  2. Set aside limes for garnish
  3. Sprinkle coarse sugar on a plate; hold each glass upside down and dip rim into sugar
  4. Set aside; disgard remaining sugar on plate
  5. In a blender, combine the lemonade concentrate and raspberries
  6. Cover and process until blended
  7. Press mixture through fine sleeve; disgard seeds
  8. Return mixture to blender; add ice, strawberries, blueberries, sugar, and tequila
  9. Cover and process until smooth
  10. Pour into prepared glasses; garnish with reserved limes
