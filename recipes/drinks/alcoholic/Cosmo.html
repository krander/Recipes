Cosmo

Yields: 1 serving

  • Ice cubes
  • 2 oz vodka
  • 2 oz cranberry juice
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • 1/2 oz triple sec
  • Cranberries (optional)
  • Lime wedge (optional)

Directions

  1. Fill shaker 3/4 full with ice
  2. Add vodka, juice, and triple sec
  3. Cover and shake for 10-15 seconds or until condensation forms on outside of shaker
  4. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass
  5. Garnish as desired
