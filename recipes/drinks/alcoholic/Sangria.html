Sangria

Yields: 9 servings

  • 1 bottle red or white Zinfandel or other dry wine (750 millimeters)
  • 2 cup lemon-lime soda
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 4 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 cup frozen raspberries
  • 1 cup frozen sliced peaches
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Directions

  1. In a pitcher, stir the wine, soda, orange juice, and sugar until sugar is dissolved
  2. Add the frozen fruit
  3. Serve over ice if desired
