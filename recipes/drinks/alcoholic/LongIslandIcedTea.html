Long Island Iced Tea

Yields: 1 serving

  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 ounce vodka
  • 1 ounce light rum
  • 1 ounce gin
  • 1 ounce triple sec
  • 1 ounce lime juice
  • 1 ounce tequila
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 2 ounce cola
  • lemon slice (optional)

Directions

  1. In a mixing glass or tumbler, combine the sugar, vodka, rum, gin, triple sec, lime juice, and tequila
  2. Stir until sugar is dissolved
  3. Place ice in a highball glass; pour in the sugar mixture
  4. Top with cola juice
  5. Garnish with lemon if desired
