Green Bean Casserole

Yields: 5 servings

  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup (10.75 oz)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 dash ground black pepper
  • 2 packages frozen green beans, cut, cooked, drained
  • 1 can fried onions (2.8 oz)

Directions

  1. Stir the soup, milk, soy sauce, black pepper, green beans, and 2/3 cup onions in a casserole dish
  2. Bake at 250 ° for 25 minutes or until hot; stir the green bean mixture
  3. Sprinkle remaining onions over the green bean mixture; bake for 5 minutes more or until onions are golden brown
