Roasted Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Yields: 6 servings

  • 1 whole garlic bulb
  • 2 2/3 cup vegetable broth
  • 5 large potatoes, cut into 1 inch pieces (about 7 1/2 cups)
  • 2 tableshooped chopped chives or green onions (optional)

Directions

  1. Cut off the top of the garlic bulb; drizzle with about 2 tablespoons of broth
  2. Wrap the bulb in aluminum foil and bake at 350 ° for 1 hour or until softened
  3. Place the broth and potatos in a 3 quart saucepan and heat to a boil over medium-high hear
  4. Reduce heat to medium; cover and cook for 10 minutes or until potatoes are tender
  5. Drain, reserving the broth
  6. Mash the potatoes with 1 1/4 cups of the broth, 2 or 3 cloves of roasted garlic, and chives (if desired)
  7. Add additional broth if needed, until desired consistency is reached
