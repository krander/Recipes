Maple Balsamic Brussels Sprouts

Serves 4

Source: My Darling Vegan

  • 4 cups brussels sprouts, halved
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 2 teaspoon maple syrup

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Wash and half the Brussels Sprouts and toss them with oil, onions, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread onto a baking sheet in a single layer and bake for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until browned on the outside and tender on the inside.
  4. Stir together balsamic vinegar and maple syrup and set aside.
  5. Remove from oven and toss with maple balsamic glaze.
