Garlic Potato Soup

Yields: 4 servings

  • 3 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 medium red potatoes, cut into cubes (about 4 cups)
  • 2 medium carrots, diced (about 1 cup)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped (about 1/2 cup)
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped (about 1/2 cup)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup instant mashed potato flakes
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped

Directions

  1. Heat the broth, garlic, potatoes, carrots, onion, and celery in a 4 quart saucepan over medium-high heat to a boil
  2. Reduce the heat to low; cover and cook for 15 minutes or until the vegetables are tender
  3. Reduce the hear to medium; stir the milk, potato flakes, and parsley in
  4. Cook until the mixture is hot and bubbling, stirring occassionally
