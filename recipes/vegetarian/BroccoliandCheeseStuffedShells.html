Broccoli and cheese stuffed shells

Yields: 6 servings

  • 1 container ricotta cheese (15 oz)
  • 1 package frozen broccoli, chopped, thawed, drained (10 oz)
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (4 oz)
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 18 jumbo shell pasta, cooked, drained
  • 1 jar pasta sauce (24 oz)

Directions

  1. Stir ricotta cheese, broccoli, 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper in a medium bowl
  2. Spoon about 2 tablespoons of the mixture into each shell
  3. Spread 1 cup of pasta sauce in shallow baking dish (13x9x2); place filled shells on the sauce
  4. Pour the remaining sauce over the shells; sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella cheese
  5. Bake at 400 ° for 25 minutes or until it's hot and bubbling
