Broccoli and noodles supreme

Yields: 5 servings

  • 3 cup uncooked medium egg noodles
  • 2 cup fresh or frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 can cream of celery (10.75 oz)
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Cook the noodles according to package; add the broccoli for the last 5 minutes of cooking time
  2. Drain the noodle mixture; return the mixture to the saucepan
  3. Stir the soup, sourcream, cheese, and black pepper in the saucepan; cook over medium hea until mixture is hot and bubbling; stirring often
