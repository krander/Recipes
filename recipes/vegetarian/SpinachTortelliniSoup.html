Spinach Tortellini Soup

Serves 4

Source: Umami Girl

  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 2 large carrots, diced
  • 2 large celery stalks, diced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 8 cup vegetable broth
  • 5 ounce baby spinach
  • 9 ounce tortellini
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • Ground black pepper
  • Grated parmesan, for serving

Directions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add onion, carrots, celery and salt and stir to coat with oil. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until beginning to soften.
  2. Stir in stock, bring to a boil, then cover and reduce heat to simmer until veggies are tender.
  3. Remove lid and add spinach and tortellini. Cook according to tortellini package instructions.
  4. Take off the heat, stir in parsley and black pepper to taste.
  5. Ladle into bowls and serve with cheese to pass at the table.
