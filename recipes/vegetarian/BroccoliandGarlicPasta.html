Broccoli and garlic penne pasta

Yields: 4 servings

  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil leaves, crushed
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cup broccoli florets
  • 4 1/2 cup penne pasta, cooked and drained
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoon parmesan cheese, grated

Directions

  1. Heat the broth, basil, black pepper, garlic, and broccoli in a skillet over medium heat to a boil
  2. Reduce the heat to low; cover and cook until the broccoli is tender-crisp
  3. Add the pasta and lemon juice; toss to coat
  4. Sprinkle paste mixture with cheese
