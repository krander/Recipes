Vegetable Lo Mein

Serves 4

Source: Kitschen Cat

  • 8 ounces lo mein egg noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cup broccoli, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon sriracha

Directions

  1. Boil noodles according to package directions, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in large skillet or wok over medium high heat. Saute garlic and onion together for 2 minutes.
  3. Whisk together soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, ground ginger, and sriracha for sauce.
  4. Pour sauce over top of vegetables and stir.
  5. Add in cooked noodles to vegetables and toss to combine.
  6. Serve!
