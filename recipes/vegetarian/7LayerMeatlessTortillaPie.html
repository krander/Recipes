7 Layer Meatless Tortilla Pie

Yields: 6 servings

  • 2 cans pinto beans, rinsed, drained (15 oz)
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder or 1 glove garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoon fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 can black beans, rinsed, drained (15 oz)
  • 1 small tomato, chopped (about 1/2 cup)
  • 7 tortillas (8-inch)
  • 8 ounce shreeded cheddar cheese (about 2 cups)

Directions

  1. Mash the pinto beans in a medium bowl with a fork
  2. Stir in 3/4 cup salsa and the garlic powder
  3. Stir in the remaining salsa, cilantro, black beans, and tomato in a medium bowl
  4. Place 1 tortilla onto a baking sheet; spread 3/4 cup pinto bean mixture over the tortilla to within 1/2 inch of the edge
  5. Top with 1/4 cup cheese; top with 1 tortilla and 2/3 cup black bean mixture
  6. Top with 1/4 cup cheese; repeat the layers twice more
  7. Top with the remaining tortilla and spread with the remaining pinto bean mixture; cover with aluminum foil
  8. Bake at 400 ° for 40 minutes or until filling it hot
  9. Uncover the pie; top with remaining cheese
  10. Cut pie into 6 wedges; serve with additional salsa and sprinkle additional cilantro if desired
