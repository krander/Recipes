Spicy Cauliflower Power Bowl

Serves 4

Source: Pumpkin and Peanut Butter

  • 1 head of cauliflower, chopped into florets
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt and pepper
  • 1/3 cup Frank's Red Hot Sauce
  • 3 tablespoon sriracha
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 1 cup quinoa, dry
  • 2 cup water
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 1 cup kale (or spinach)
  • 1 1/2 cup chickpeas, cooked or canned
  • 1 avocado, diced

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Drizzle cauliflower with one tbsp of olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread evenly over a baking sheet and roast for 30 minutes, tossing once or twice throughout.
  4. Boil 2 cups of water with 1 cup of dry quinoa, then turn down to a simmer, and cover.
  5. Cook for 15-20 minutes, or until all the water is gone and the quinoa is fluffy.
  6. In a small sauce pan, whisk together Frank's Red Hot, sriracha, butter, and milk over medium heat.
  7. Once the butter is melted and the sauce is hot, turn off heat and set aside.
  8. In a large skillet, drizzle 1 tbsp of olive oil and add in diced yellow onion. Cook for 5-6 minutes, or until onion is translucent.
  9. Add in roasted cauliflower and top with the spicy sauce. Mix to coat all the cauliflower and onion in the sauce.
  10. Time to assemble your bowls! Start with a big scoop of quinoa, then a handful of chickpeas and kale. Add in a large helping of spicy cauliflower and some diced avocado. Serve!
