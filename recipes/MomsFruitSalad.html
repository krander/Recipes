Mom's Fruit Salad

Serves 7

  • 20 oz can pineapple chunks, drained
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 11 oz can mandarin oranges
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1 kiwi, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon lemon zest

Directions

  1. Combine fruits
  2. Combine liquids once ready to be served
